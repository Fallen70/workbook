Find symbolic links

find . -type l -ls

Find symbolic links to a specific target

find . -lname link_target Note that link_target may contain wildcard characters.

Find broken symbolic links

find -L . -type l -ls The -L option instructs find to follow symbolic links, unless when broken.

Find & replace broken symbolic links

find -L . -type l -delete -exec ln -s new_target {} \;

More find examples can be found here: https://hamwaves.com/find/
